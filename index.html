Beware of the Zombies!

If you have access to this site, it would mean that the world is in the middle of a disaster: a ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE. Some might tackle the fact that the cause of the outbreak must be accurately identified before declaring its name. Nevertheless, time passes while defining the reason, and it is better to know more than learning less.
Let’s talk about the origin of a zombie. Traditionally, the zombie is a mythological creature that revives from death. The main characters of zombies are the loss of rationality and excessive aggression toward living things. In many media, zombie occurs via the outbreak of dangerous chemical spill causing a mutant virus that turns people into living undead. It is ridiculous that what we enjoyed as a fantasy has become the reality.

PREPARE THE APOCALPSE

Now the question is, what do I need to do to be safe from the zombies?
First, you should start by making the emergency kit. This kit would save your life when you endure the attack at home or when you move to a zombie-free refugee camp. It is not a good idea to weigh your luggage, yet I hope you prepare considering as many scenarios as possible.
Below is the basic list of the emergency kit.

THINGS TO
BE INCLUDED
IN YOUR
EMERGENCY KIT
  • Water :1 gallon per person per day
  • Food :conservation food like canned food
  • Medications and First Aid Supplies :includes prescription and non-prescription meds
  • Tools and Supplies :utility knife, lighter, beddings, radio, etc.
  • Important Documents :copies of your driver’s license, passport, and birth certificate

Your Action is important.

Do not give up. Stay Alive!

STEP 1

Prepare the Shelter

A food, clothing, and shelter are necessary elements for living. You cannot watch 24 hours around, and the walls definitely can protect you from various dangers from natural disasters to human disasters.

STEP 2

Reserve the Resources

Self-sufficiency is essential in apocalypse states. Acts like collecting water with various containers from the rooftop and stocking up the preserved food in the nearby stores would improve your survival rates.

STEP 3

Plan the
evacuation route

It would be best if your shelter holds on until the case is resolved though people do not know what will happen. To safely escape from the catastrophe, you need to know the possible evacuation route to other safe places.

STEP 4

Contact the locals

CDC and the government authorities would not abandon citizens. There are always people who are striving to find the solution. Do not hesitate to contact various local support such as the police and your local zombie response team.
